About SHL and Associates

SHL & Associates Lawyers is a Sydney based law firm, providing a wide range of legal services to Australian as well as international clients. We specialise in commercial litigation, property law, commercial law, family law and immigration law. Our sustained growth since our inception is a testament to our professionalism, expertise and our commitment to our clients. At SHL & Associates Lawyers, we look after your best interest.
